## Why price monitoring is critical today

In ecommerce and retail, prices change multiple times a day. A competitor can drop their price on Monday morning and you won't find out until Friday — by which point you've already lost sales.

Price monitoring stops being a competitive advantage and becomes a basic operational requirement.

## What you should track

Not all prices are equally important to your business. Focus on:

1. **High-volume products** — where small price differences have a big impact on volume
2. **Categories with many competitors** — where price comparison is most frequent
3. **Tight-margin products** — where getting it wrong costs the most
